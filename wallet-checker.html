The Be More Bitcoin Wallet Rating

A score out of 10 based on activity, balance stability, and transaction history. High scores indicate established, active wallets; low scores may signal new or dormant ones.

  • Inactive wallets lose score over time.
  • Score factors: tx count, recent activity, balance changes.
  • Timestamps are approximate (±1-2 hours due to block timing).
  • Balances in full BTC (unconfirmed tx excluded).
