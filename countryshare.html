How many Bitcoin should your country own?

Based on Population

Simplifing slightly, assuming all Bitcoin are mined, and none are lost, the number of bitcoin each country should have, (or held by it's population), is shown below.

There are a few countries right now sitting quite comfortably, in a bitcoin ecconomy they could catapult from one of the poorest to one of the riches countries in the world. Others will potnetially have to pay more to catchup later or fight to maintain the current system. There are multiple ways a country could end up in a stronger position through Bitcoin.

  • The government themselves hold Bitcoin.
  • Bitcoin regulation encourages development of infrastructure and a mindset of holding some of your wealth in Bitcoin.
  • Government controls, restrictions and currency stability highlight the need for Bitcoin and drive adoption.

All three are happening right now, and at different rates, in a way, some of the most stable countries could end up being harmed the most by this their population are the slowed to adapt.
